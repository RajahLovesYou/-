Reflection on Professional Conduct

Professional conduct     

My understanding of professionalism in the field of IT, mostly focusing on the BCS Code of Conduct, I feel like I've grasped about 70% of its essence if i was to grade my gain from the module. While I'm aware of the importance of professionalism, there are areas where I could enhance my comprehension further. Nonetheless, I appreciate the insights gained thus far.

Regarding my behavior during the module, I acknowledge that there were instances where I exhibited laziness. However, despite these tendencies, I found solace in the fact that my teachers were always available to provide support. Their guidance and encouragement played a pivotal role in ensuring that my studies remained on track. In hindsight, I realize the significance of their presence and how it contributed to maintaining a level of consistency in my academic pursuits. Moving forward, I aim to address my lapses in diligence while continuing to leverage the support available to me.
